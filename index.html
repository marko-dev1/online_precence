Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

How fast can I really get my website?

We guarantee to have your first draft ready within 24 hours of receiving all your content and information. Most clients have their final website live within 48 hours.

What if I don't like the design?

Each plan includes revision rounds (1 for Starter, 2 for Professional, 3 for E-Commerce). We'll work with you until you're completely satisfied with the design.

Do I need to provide anything?

We'll need your business information, logo, photos, and any specific content you want included. Don't have professional photos? We can help source high-quality stock images.

What about ongoing maintenance?

Our ksh 500/month hosting plan includes all maintenance, security updates, backups, and technical support. You can focus on your business while we handle the technical details.

Can I update the website myself?

Absolutely! We provide easy-to-use tools for basic updates. For more complex changes, our team is available to help (additional fees may apply for major updates).
